Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Place slices of mozzarella on top.
  5. Add fresh basil leaves.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
Enjoy your homemade Margherita Pizza!
